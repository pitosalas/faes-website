# Our History

## Founding

FAES was formed in 2010 by Pito Salas in honor of his father Abram Edgardo Salas. The initial board members included Pito Salas, Margarita Salas, Virginia, and Janice. Our advisory board included Patricia Salas, Larry Salas, Harlan Cohen, and Chris Salas. Since formation we have given out an average of XCG 100,000 every year.

## Grant Process (Internal Notes)

We have two kinds of grants:

1. **Pilot grants** of XCG 2,500 to XCG 10,000 for recipients we are getting to know. We do a basic amount of due diligence, with almost no paperwork or reporting requested.
2. **Primary grants** of XCG 10,000 up to XCG 100,000 for recipients we have gotten to know and are really comfortable with. We make only a few Primary grants a year. We will invite you by email to send a "Letter of Interest" to begin the process, followed by our own due diligence, reference checking, and follow up. See the [grants page](/grants) for awarded grants.
