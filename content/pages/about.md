# About the Foundation

The Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas was established in honor of Abram Edgardo Salas, whose life exemplified dedication to family, community, and lifelong learning.

## Who We Are

We are a small, independent foundation committed to making a positive impact through targeted grants. Our work is guided by the values that Abram Edgardo Salas embodied throughout his life.

## How We Operate

The foundation is managed by a small board of trustees who review grant applications annually. We prioritize transparency and simplicity in everything we do.
