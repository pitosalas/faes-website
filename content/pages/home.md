# Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas

<div class="bilingual-grid">
	<section class="lang-panel">
		<h3>Who We Are</h3>
		<p>We are a grant-making organization established in Curaçao. We provide support to a variety of non-profits and foundations in Curaçao. We focus on small organizations that do not necessarily have the attention of large institutional donors because they are new, small, and less well known.</p>
		<h3>Our Process</h3>
		<p>Our process is grassroots, through personal connections and recommendations. All our grantees came to our attention through a referral or recommendation directly to our team in Curaçao and abroad.</p>
		<h3>What To Do</h3>
		<p>If your organization seems to match up with our goals, reach out to one of our board members through your network and connections. We will seek a referral from inside our network of advisors, board members, and other grantees. Once the connection is made we will talk and explain the next steps.</p>
	</section>
	<section class="lang-panel">
		<h3>Ken Nos Ta</h3>
		<p>Nos ta un organizashon ku ta otorga subsidio, establese na Korsou, pa duna sosten finansiero na diferente organisashonnan sin fines di lucro i fundashonnan na Korsou. Nos ta pone enfoke riba organisashonnan chiki ku no semper ta haña atenshon di donor institushonal grandi pasobra nan ta nobo, chiki i menos konosi.</p>
		<h3>Nos Proseso</h3>
		<p>Nos proseso ta basa riba kontaktonan personal i rekomendashonnan. Tur nos beneficiario a yega na nos atenshon via un referensia of rekomendashon direktamente na nos tim na Korsou i na exterior.</p>
		<h3>Kiko Bo Por Hasi</h3>
		<p>Si bo organisashon ta parse ku ta kuadra ku nos metanan, tuma kontakto ku un di nos miembro di hunta via bo ret i konekshonnan. Nos lo buska un referensia for di den nos ret di asesornan, miembro di hunta i otro beneficiarionan. Ora e konekshon ta hasi, nos lo papia i splika e siguiente pasonan.</p>
	</section>
</div>
