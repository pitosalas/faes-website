# Our Mission

The Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas exists to support individuals and organizations that make a meaningful difference in their communities.

## Values

- **Integrity** — We act honestly and transparently in all that we do.
- **Community** — We believe in the strength of people supporting one another.
- **Education** — We support learning and growth at every stage of life.
- **Opportunity** — We seek to open doors for those who need it most.

## Grant Philosophy

We award grants where they can have the greatest impact. We favor projects that are concrete, achievable, and rooted in the communities they serve.
