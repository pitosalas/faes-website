<br/>
**Treasurer**
<br/>
Virginia is the Treasurer of FAES. She joined FAES at the request of the late Mrs. Marguin Salas, mother of the founder of FAES Pito Salas. She is a retired financial professional with more than 40 years of experience in the banking sector, both in Curaçao and Internationally. Throughout her career she has combined professional excellence with a strong commitment to making a positive impact on the community of Curaçao
