# Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas

### Who We Are

:photo "punda1.jpg", "Downtown Curacao waterfront", 200, right

We are a grant-making organization started in Curaçao in 2008. We support many non-profits and foundations in Curaçao. We have been giving grants in Curacao for more than 10 years. During that time, we have given almost XCG 500,000!

We are a small team and do our work quietly. We focus on small organizations that don't get attention from large donors because they are new, small, and less well known.

### Our Process

Our process is personal, based on connections and recommendations. All our grantees were recommended to us directly by people in our network in Curaçao and abroad.

### What To Do

If your organization matches our goals, reach out to one of our board members through your network and connections. We will ask for a referral from our network of advisors, board members, and other grantees. Once the connection is made, we will talk and explain the next steps.

# About the Foundation

Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas is a grant-making organization based in Curaçao. We were founded in 2008 and since then we have given almost XCG 500.000 to small non-profits and foundations that larger donors often miss.

## History

:photo "bhs.jpg", "Boekhandel Salas where it all started", 300, right

FAES was formed in 2010 by Pito Salas in honor of his father Abram Edgardo Salas. The first board members included Pito Salas, Margarita Salas, Virginia Evers-Kleinmoedig, and Janice Godschalk. Our advisory board included Patricia Salas, Larry Salas, and Harlan Cohen. [Meet the current board](/board).

## How We Operate

The foundation is managed by a small board of directors who review grant applications every year. Our criteria and values are described on our [mission page](/mission).

[ENGLISH]
# Our Mission and Process

### Who We Are

[Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas](/about) helps organizations that make a real difference in Curaçao. We find, meet with, and support non-profit programs across the island.

### Who We Support

We look for organizations who directly help people in need. We support programs that focus on programs fighting poverty, homelessness, drug addiction, illiteracy, hunger, child abuse, and alcohol abuse. We also support children's health, safety, and wellbeing. We also work with the Jewish community of Curaçao. We focus on helping the community stay strong, and on preserving their history, culture, buildings, and artifacts.

:photo "punda1.jpg", "Downtown Curacao waterfront", 200, right

### What We Look For

We value partner organizations that are small, hands-on, and doing the work themselves on the ground. We look for competence, professionalism, expertise, transparency, and high ethical standards.

### Our Grants

**Pilot grants** of XCG 2,500 to XCG 10,000 for recipients we are getting to know. We do basic research on these organizations, with very little paperwork or reporting required.

**Primary grants** of XCG 10,000 up to XCG 100,000 for recipients we know well and trust. We request a "Letter of Interest" to begin the process, followed by our own research, reference checking, and follow up.

# Nos Mision i Proseso

### Kiko Nos Ta

[Fundashon Abram Edgardo Salas](/about) ta yuda organisashonnan ku ta hasi un diferensia real na Kòrsou. Nos ta buska, topa ku, i apoyá programanan sin fin di lucro pa henter e isla.

### Ken Nos Ta Apoyá

Nos ta buska organisashonnan ku ta yuda direktamente hendenan ku mester di yuda. Nos ta apoyá programanan ku ta lubra kòntra pobreza, falta di kas, adikshon na droga, analfabetismo, hamber, abuso di mucha, i abuso di alkohòl. Nos tambe ta apoyá salú, siguridad, i bienestar di mucha. Nos tambe ta traha ku e komunidad judío di Kòrsou. Nos ta enfoka nos mes riba yuda e komunidad keda fuerte, i riba preservá nan historia, kultura, edifisionan, i artifaktonan.

### Kiko Nos Ta Buska

:photo "punda1.jpg", "Downtown Curacao waterfront", 200, right

Nos ta valorá organisashonnan partner ku ta chikitu, práktikonan, i ku ta hasi e trabou nan mes riba e tereno. Nos ta buska kompetenshia, profesionalismo, ekspertisia, transparensia, i estandarnan étikonan haltu.

### Nos Beurs

**Beurs piloto** di XCG 2.500 pa XCG 10.000 pa resepientnan ku nos ta kuminsá konosé. Nos ta hasi investigashon básiko riba e organisashonnan aki, ku masha poko papelnan òf reporte rekerí.

**Beurs primario** di XCG 10.000 te ku XCG 100.000 pa resepientnan ku nos ta konosé bon i ku nos ta konfía aden. Nos ta pidi un "Karta di Interes" pa kuminsá e proseso, sigú pa nos propio investigashon, chèk di referenshia, i seguimento.
